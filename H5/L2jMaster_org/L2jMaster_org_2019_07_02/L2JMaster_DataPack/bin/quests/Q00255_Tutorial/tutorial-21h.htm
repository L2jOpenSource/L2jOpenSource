[Dark Mystic Class Transfer]:
A Dark Mystic who has reached level 20 may change their class to a Dark Wizard or a Shillien Oracle. A character may undertake the class transfer quest at level 18; if successful, they will be able to use more specialized and powerful skills than before. In order to become a Dark Wizard, you must first meet the Witch Varika, and in order to become a Shillien Oracle, you must first meet Magister Sidra. The Witch Varika can be found near the Altar of Rites in the Dark Elf Village, and Magister Sidra can be found in the Town of Gludio. Think carefully about which class you want to select, then seek out the appropriate NPC.
