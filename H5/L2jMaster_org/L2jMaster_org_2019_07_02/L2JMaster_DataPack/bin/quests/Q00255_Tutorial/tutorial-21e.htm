[Orc Mystic Class Transfer]:
An Orc Mystic who has reached level 20 may change their class to an Orc Shaman. A character may undertake the class transfer quest at level 18; if successful, they will be able to use more specialized and powerful skills than before. In order to become an Orc Shaman, you must first go to the Orc Village and meet Tataru Zu Hestui. May this class transfer offer you even more enjoyable adventures!
