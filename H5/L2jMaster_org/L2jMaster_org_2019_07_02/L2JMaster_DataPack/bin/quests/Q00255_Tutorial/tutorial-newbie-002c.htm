Go to the Elven Village and speak with Sentinel Kendell. He'll ask that you defeat the Kaboo Orcs who invaded the Elven Forest.
Complete this quest and you'll receive either a Red Sunset Sword or a Staff of Red Glow, both of which are excellent weapons! You'll also receive a Soulshot for Beginners or a Spiritshot for Beginners.
[Penalty for Dying]

From now on you'll be penalized for dying. You must avoid death in order to preserve your Exp.
