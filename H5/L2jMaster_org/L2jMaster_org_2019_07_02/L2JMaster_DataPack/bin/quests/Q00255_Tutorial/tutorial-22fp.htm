[Elven Scout Class Transfer]:
In order to become a Plainswalker, The Elven Scout must successfully complete three quests. The Trial of the Seeker can only be undertaken by a player of level 35 or above. This quest begins by meeting with Master Dufner of the Town of Giran. The Testimony of Life can only be undertaken by a player of level 37 or above. This quest begins by meeting with Master Cardien of the Town of Dion. Finally, the Test of the Searcher can be undertaken by a player of level 39 or above. This quest begings by meeting with Master Luther of Hunters Village.
