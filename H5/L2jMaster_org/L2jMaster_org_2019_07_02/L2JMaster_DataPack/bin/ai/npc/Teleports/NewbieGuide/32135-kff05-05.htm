Newbie Guide:
Good question! Have you talked to the inhabitants of the town? Many of them need help. When you click Quest, they will ask a favor of you.
No matter how dire their need may be, however, they will never ask for something you cannot do. If someone hesitates to ask a favor of you, it tells you that the quest may put you in danger since you are less experienced.
Finally, there are two types of quests: one-time quests, which can only be undertaken once, and repetitive quests, which may be undertaken multiple times.
