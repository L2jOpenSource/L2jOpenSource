Newbie Guide:
Welcome! Confused by how to begin your career as an adventurer?
I and my fellow Newbie Guides can help you.
In addition to giving you helpful tips, we will also assign you several missions to help you gain experience, acquire equipment and receive rewards.
For your first mission, go help Darin. To find him, just follow the arrow above your head. When you have completed the task he gives you, come see me for your next mission.
Before you go, is there anything else I can do for you?
Ask for advice.
Find NPC.
Receive Blessing of Protection.
Teleport to another village.
View available Support Magic.
Ask about Newbie Shadow Weapons and Armor.
