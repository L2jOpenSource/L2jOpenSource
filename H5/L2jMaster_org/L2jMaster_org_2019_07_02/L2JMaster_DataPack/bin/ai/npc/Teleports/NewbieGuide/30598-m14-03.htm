Newbie Guide:
Have you heard of the Obelisk of Victory? Travel north from town for a little while, then head west until you see a tower. Created by the Elves and called the Obelisk of Victory, it's considered a good place to hunt for anyone past level 7. For someone of your level, focus on battling Orcs, Orc Warriors and Werewolves.
But be careful when fighting Orc Warriors. Unlike the other monsters you've faced so far, they are aggressive and will attack you on sight!
Once you pass level 10, head south to the magic school or north to the Obelisk of Victory and try your skill against Werewolf Hunters, Chieftains or Stone Golems.
Go back.
