Newbie Guide:
If you want to travel the world, go to Hierarch Kekropus. He knows a hidden truth about our race, which I have heard that he will only reveal to a fully-grown Kamael like you.
Also seek Vice Hierarch Perwan in the Cave of Souls. He needs someone to eradicate the Crimson Spiders who have infested Mimir's Forest, and I think you might be ready for such an important task.
Finally, receive a quest from Trader Erinu in town. He is collecting materials for making accessories, but the materials he needs can only be found in the Nornil's Cave. Everyone says that it is too dangerous for a novice to go, so Erinu has been searching for an experienced warrior.
