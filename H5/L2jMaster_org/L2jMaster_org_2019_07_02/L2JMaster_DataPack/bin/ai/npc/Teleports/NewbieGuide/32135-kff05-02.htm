Newbie Guide:
Unless someone provides you with something better, you will likely start with the basic weapon and armor commonly distributed to beginners.
You can upgrade your weapons and armor by defeating monsters and taking theirs. If you hole some trumps, you can also obtain items like a pair of Cloth Shoes, Apprentice's Shoes or a Cloth Cap.
You initially received two types of sword: a Dagger and a Squire's Sword. A Dagger is quick but has a low accuracy; a Squire's Sword is slower but more accurate. You can also use different skills depending on the type of weapon wielded.
Train hard and return to me when you have reached level 6. Then I will teach you more about weaponry.
